# Case Studies

Three high-risk districts were selected from the latest district acquisition risk output.
Each memo is grounded in transaction risk, merchant saturation, and replacement-candidate logic.

## 1. 서초구

- Verdict: `Very High` (78.9)
- Core objections: pricing looks stretched relative to district peers; merchant saturation is elevated; recent transaction pricing is unstable
- Latest month: `202603` with 21 trades and a median `sqm` price of 2633.1 (10k KRW)
- Six-month price change: +112.9%
- Six-month transaction change: -53.3%
- Saturation signal: 2160.7 stores per admin-dong, food-store share 17.1%
- Sample reliability: `High`
- Dominant categories: 경영 컨설팅업:2710 | 변호사:2678 | 부동산 중개/대리업:2094
- Why the model flagged it: price burden is elevated, with 6m price growth at 104.2%; price volatility has been high in recent months
- Lower-risk substitutes: 금천구 | 관악구 | 양천구
- Why substitutes look better: lower overall acquisition risk; lighter merchant saturation | lower overall acquisition risk; stronger transaction liquidity; lighter merchant saturation
- Field checks before underwriting: verify whether recent asking prices still clear at transaction levels; walk the main admin-dong corridors and count duplicate tenant formats; review large outlier trades and separate whole-building deals from strata units
